About

Hi, my name is Shwarnali Bhattacharjee, and I'm an aspiring GIS professional with over 5 years of experience in the GIS field. I have a strong passion for cartography.

I'm currently pursuing a Master's degree in Applied Geography with a concentration in GIS and remote sensing at University of North Texas, Denton, Texas. Additionally, I earned my Bachelor's degree in Geography and Environment at Shahjalal University of Science and Technology, Sylhet, Bangladesh. Throughout my academic career, I developed a deep appreciation for the power of maps to convey complex information and tell compelling stories.

I'm passionate about GIS to use data-driven solutions to help organizations make informed decisions. I have a diverse set of GIS skills, including data management, spatial analysis, and geospatial visualization. I have worked with various GIS software, including ArcGIS, QGIS, and CARTO and enjoy experimenting with different workflows and data sources to see what works best for each project.

As I prepare to enter the field of GIS as a professional, I am excited to use my cartographic skills to help organizations communicate their data in meaningful ways. I believe that cartography is a crucial part of the GIS process, as it allows us to visualize spatial patterns and relationships in a clear and digestible manner.

I have had the opportunity to work on a variety of GIS and cartography projects during my studies, including creating interactive maps for mangrove forest, building 3D urban models, assessing hydrology of city parks, analyzing land use land cover change of a freshwater wetland, and digitizing scanned and pdf maps. I enjoy exploring new cartographic techniques and experimenting with different mapping styles to create unique and admirable maps.

My aim as an aspiring GIS professional and cartographer is to leverage my expertise to support organizations in solving complex problems and presenting their data in engaging and user-friendly ways. My academic background has enabled me to acquire strong communication, leadership, problem-solving, creativity, and critical thinking skills, which I believe will be invaluable in my future career. Moving forward, I am committed to further developing these competencies and utilizing them to make meaningful contributions in my field.
