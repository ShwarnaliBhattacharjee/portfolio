Project information

  • Category: ArcGIS StoryMap
  • Project Date: Summer, 2022
  • Tools Used: ArcGIS Pro, ArcGIS Online, Adobe Illustrator
  • Project URL: https://storymaps.arcgis.com/Sundarbans

I'm excited to share with you an ArcGIS StoryMap that I created to explore the beauty and significance of the Sundarbans - the largest mangrove forest in the world. With this project, my goal was to provide a comprehensive overview of the ecoregion, protected areas, wildlife, plants, threats, conservation efforts, and management strategies that are essential to preserving this unique and important ecosystem.

To ensure that the content of this StoryMap was both engaging and informative, I collaborated with a PhD student from the Department of Geography and Environmental Systems at UMBC. His expertise and insights helped me to present a well-rounded and holistic view of the Sundarbans, and I'm grateful for his contribution.

I'm proud to share that this StoryMap was recognized as a runner-up in the 2022 ArcGIS StoryMap Competition, specifically in the Nature and Physical Science category. It's an honor to have this work acknowledged, and I hope that it inspires others to learn more about the importance of protecting and preserving our planet's precious natural resources.
