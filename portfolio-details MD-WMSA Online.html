Project information

  • Category: Web App
  • Project date: July 2023 - September 2023
  • Tool Used: ArcGIS Online Map Viewer, Experience Builder Developer Edition, ArcGIS Server, ArcGIS Enterprise Portal
  • Web App URL: Maryland Water Management Strategy Areas Online

To support water resource planning and improve public access to environmental data, I developed an interactive web mapping application for Maryland Water Management Strategy Areas (MD WMSA). Using ArcGIS Experience Builder Developer Edition, ArcGIS Server, and the ArcGIS Enterprise Portal, I created a user-friendly interface that allows both agency staff and the public to explore, analyze, and download spatial data related to water management areas across the state. The tool integrates authoritative GIS layers and offers dynamic filtering, pop-up info panels, and responsive design for accessibility on multiple devices. By streamlining access to up-to-date spatial data and enabling custom analysis within the browser, this project enhanced interagency collaboration and empowered citizens with greater transparency around state water management strategies.
