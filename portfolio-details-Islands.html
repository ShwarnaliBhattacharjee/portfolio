Project information

  • Category: Cartography
  • Project date: February, 2023
  • Tool Used: ArcGIS Pro

This map showcases the stunning geography of Caribbean Islands and its surrounding areas with striking visual elements. The vignette draws the viewer's attention to the central area while creating a dramatic contrast with the surrounding white space. The sculpted terrain gives a sense of depth and texture, inviting exploration. The water is represented with delicate lines that create a sense of motion and fluidity. The paper texture adds a warm and organic feel, enhancing the map's overall aesthetic. Finally, the overview map provides a unique and captivating perspective of the region with a mesmerizing glassy style, provides a unique and captivating perspective of the region. I made it for fun.
